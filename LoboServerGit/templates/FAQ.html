FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)


What is the time complexity of merge sort?
- BEST: Ω(nlog(n)), AVERAGE: θ(nlog(n)), WORST: O(nlog(n))

What is the time complexity of quick sort?
- BEST: Ω(nlog(n)), AVERAGE: θ(nlog(n)), WORST: O(n^2)

What is the time complexity of insertion sort?
- BEST: Ω(n), AVERAGE: θ(n^2), WORST: O(n^2)

What is the time complexity of selection sort?
- BEST: Ω(n^2), AVERAGE: θ(n^2), WORST: O(n^2)

What is the time complexity of heap sort?
- BEST: Ω(nlog(n)), AVERAGE: θ(nlog(n)), WORST: O(nlog(n))

What is the time complexity of bubble sort?
- BEST: Ω(n), AVERAGE: θ(n^2), WORST: O(n^2)

What is the order of complexity?
- O(1) < O(log(N)) < O(N) < O(Nlog(N)) < O(N^2) < O(n^3) < O(2^N) < O(N!)
